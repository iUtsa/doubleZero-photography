Payment & Booking Information

We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees. All packages include professional editing and high-resolution digital images.

Booking Process

50% retainer due at booking to secure your date. Remaining balance due 7 days before your session or event.

Payment Methods

We accept cash, check, credit cards, Venmo, PayPal, and Zelle for your convenience.

Cancellation Policy

Full refund for cancellations 30+ days prior. 50% refund for 14-30 days. No refund within 14 days of session.

Delivery Timeline

Regular sessions: 1-2 weeks. Weddings: 4-6 weeks. Mini sessions: 3-5 business days. Rush delivery available.
